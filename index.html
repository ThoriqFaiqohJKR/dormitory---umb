Welcome to the Dormitory Management System

The Dormitory Management System is an integrated platform that simplifies dormitory administration for international students through a Submission App (Facility Report, Dormitory Exit Permit, Visitor Permits, Meeting Room Permit, Dormitory Laboratory Visitors) and Data Management (International Student Data, Facility Report Monitoring, Dormitory Laboratory Visitors Report), enabling transparent, efficient, and accountable management of facilities and student activities.
